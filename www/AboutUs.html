KOUA


In Jalisco, the craft industry suffers from adverce conditions, because this market is non-profitable, according to our studies. As a consequence, there is a lack of cultural knowledge, lack of support, and the absence of handcrafts in a fair trade; making the artisans to have a poor personal, social and economic development. KOUA is a mobile application that aims to create an easy, educational and fast link between Mexican artisans and buyers through a platform with cultural information and photographs of the different articles. The goal of KOUA is to encourage the purchase and sale of Mexican handcrafts.

We hope that you enjoy the app, as well as it is helpful for all of you.
